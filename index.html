Hello!I’m Raktim Chatterjee a self-taught Software Developer,Competitive Programmer and AI Expert.First and foremost,I love writing code.Ever since writing my first program in C++ and manipulating it to produce a desired output,I have been obsessed with the idea of using softwares and codes to solve practical problems.Software Engineering is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving.I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world.

I started in my middle school and started growing from there.For my first professional project,I was given the opportunity to develop a Retail Visual Analytics that helps retail stores to gain insights into customer behavior within store through a simple data processing cycle that builds upon itself over time. The plug-and-play technology collects and analyses data locally and in the cloud, providing the information and tools that are needed to optimize a retail space, and providing the ability to immediately see the effects of those changes.Most recently I have been working on my startup company,that provides solutions and services in the fields of fintech,agritech and smart-communications.

Currently I’m UG-Computer Science Engineering student of Guru Nanak Institute Of Technology,Kolkata alongside being a full-time freelancer working on software development projects.My hobbies include speedcubing,playing chess,solving competitive programming problems on dedicated websites and playing Football.
